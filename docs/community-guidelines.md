# 🔊 BASS PHONK — Community Guidelines

Welcome to the **BASS PHONK** community! Our platform is a dedicated ecosystem celebrating Phonk music, drift culture, art, and the creators who drive it. To maintain a safe, inspiring, and respectful space for everyone, all users must follow these guidelines.

---

## 1. Respect Creators and Listeners

Phonk is an expressive, diverse culture, but we do not tolerate hate or harassment.
- **Be Respectful**: Treat artists, curators, and other listeners with courtesy. Constructive criticism on tracks is welcome, but personal attacks, insults, or harassment will result in account suspension.
- **Zero Tolerance for Hate Speech**: Any comments, profiles, or playlist titles containing racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, religious intolerance, or xenophobia are strictly banned.
- **Protect Privacy**: Do not share other users' personal information (doxxing), private photos, or messages without their explicit consent.

## 2. Music and Upload Policies

If you upload tracks to BASS PHONK as an artist:
- **Respect Copyright**: Only upload music that you have created, own the rights to, or have explicit permissions to distribute. Uploading unlicensed popular tracks as your own is a violation of our Terms of Service and will lead to track removal and potential ban.
- **Proper Labeling**: Accurately categorize your tracks using the appropriate Phonk genre tags (Brazilian, Drift, Memphis, Cowbell, Dark, etc.) and include the correct BPM.
- **No Spam Uploads**: Do not upload the same track multiple times, or upload low-quality loops/noise purely to flood the feed.

## 3. Keep Comments and Community Sections Clean

Our community sections (comments, profiles, feeds) are for talking about music and sharing the drift aesthetic.
- **No Self-Promotion Spam**: Do not spam other artists' comment sections with links to your own tracks, social media, or commercial services.
- **No Link Spamming**: Sharing malicious, phishing, or advertising links in comments is strictly prohibited.
- **Age Appropriateness**: While Phonk aesthetic can be gritty and dark, community comment sections must remain free of graphic, sexually explicit, or highly violent text/images.

## 4. Moderation and Enforcement

We monitor reports actively to protect the community.
- **Report Violations**: If you see comments, tracks, or profiles violating these guidelines, use the in-app "Report" button to flag it for our moderation team.
- **Mod Actions**: Depending on the severity of the violation, our team may:
  1. Issue a warning.
  2. Edit or delete the violating content (comments, playlist names).
  3. Temporarily suspend account access.
  4. Permanently ban the account and delete all uploaded tracks.

Thank you for helping us keep **BASS PHONK** the ultimate ecosystem for phonk music and culture! Keep it loud, keep it heavy. 🚗💨
